Team 2B

Gamification of MetPsy App


MetPsy is an online coaching platform founded by Rachel Brown and Ally Houston, offering personalized plans that combine dietary and lifestyle changes to improve mental health through metabolic balance.

Problem Statement

Design an innovative gamification system for the MetPsy app to engage users beyond live sessions, incorporating mood scales and biological measurements, while ensuring real-world impact and improved mental health outcomes.
